Cybersecurity Safety Tips

  • 1. Use Strong Passwords: Avoid common words; use a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.
  • 2. Beware of Phishing: Never click suspicious links or attachments in emails.
  • 3. Enable Two-Factor Authentication: Adds extra protection for your accounts.
  • 4. Keep Software Updated: Security patches fix critical vulnerabilities.
  • 5. Use Secure Wi-Fi: Avoid public networks for banking or private activities.
